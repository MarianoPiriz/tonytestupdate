ABOUT

Following a lifetime of love for music and fashion, Tony joined the entertainment scene in 2012. The journey continued in 2016, when he moved from his hometown, Buenos Aires, to NYC. He is an eclectic artist who loves to spin vinyl records. In his collection you will find many genres such as, DeepHouse/House/Techno/Soul/Funk/Disco/Jazz/ R&B/HipHop and rare music. He holds residencies at The Williamsburg Hotel and Monomono NYC. You can listen to him monthly on Loose Fm, London and Radio Deep, Switzerland. Tony also manages a group of emerging DJ's, musicians and artists as founder of DiscothequeNYC.
